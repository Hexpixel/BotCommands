-- List of commands for Pybot --

'!hello' - say hello to the bot
'!bot' - gets the bot's attention
'!flip' - Heads or Tails
'!color' - prints a random color
'!number' - prints a random integer between 0 and 100,000
'!SAY' - testing
'!AMIADMIN' - testing
'!purge' - delete all message from channel (developing)
